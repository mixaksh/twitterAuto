# The Ultimate Growthhacking Toolkit for Twitter

## What it does:

1. Automatically follow people who:
  1. Retweet your tweet (Native retweets. Not quotes!)
  2. Favorite your tweet
  3. Follow you

2. Favorite tweets mentioning you or quoting you.
